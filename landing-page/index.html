How it works

One add-on. Total upgrade.

SmartDrive Assist attaches to any standard manual wheelchair in three modular layers — no tools, no permanent changes, no new chair.

01
Clip it on

The entire SmartDrive system mounts onto your existing wheelchair frame — clamp-based, fully reversible, compatible with all standard manual chairs. Zero chair modifications required.

02
Sensors take over

IR and ultrasonic sensors constantly scan the environment — detecting obstacles, drop-offs like stairs and curbs, and incline angles — feeding real-time data to the onboard controller.

03
You stay in control

Switch between electric and manual modes anytime. Use voice commands, the Bluetooth remote, or the high-contrast UI — while SmartDrive handles safety automatically in the background.

Flexibility
Manual / Electric Toggle

Seamless switching between modes — electric for convenience, manual as backup. Always your choice, always instant.

Sensors
Obstacle Detection

IR sensors detect obstacles ahead and automatically slow or stop the chair before any impact.

Emergency
SOS Alerts

One-button emergency alert triggers instant notification to all pre-set emergency contacts.

Power
Auto-Recharging

A dynamo motor harvests kinetic energy while the chair is pushed manually — self-charging in motion, no plug needed.

Sensors
Edge Detection

Ultrasonic sensors detect drop-offs like steps and curbs, preventing dangerous falls before they happen.
