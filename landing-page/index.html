Features

Built different.
Engineered smarter.

Nine smart capabilities in one modular add-on — no chair replacement, no rewiring, no compromise.

Flexibility
Manual / Electric Toggle

Seamless switching between modes — electric for convenience, manual as backup. Always your choice, always instant.

Sensors
Obstacle Detection

IR sensors detect obstacles ahead and automatically slow or stop the chair before any impact.

Emergency
SOS Alerts

One-button emergency alert triggers instant notification to all pre-set emergency contacts.

Power
Auto-Recharging

A dynamo motor harvests kinetic energy while the chair is pushed manually — self-charging in motion, no plug needed.

Sensors
Edge Detection

Ultrasonic sensors detect drop-offs like steps and curbs, preventing dangerous falls before they happen.

How it works

One add-on. Total upgrade.

Attaches to any standard manual wheelchair in three modular layers — no tools, no permanent changes.

01
Clip it on

The entire SmartDrive Assist system mounts onto your existing frame — clamp-based, fully reversible, compatible with all standard manual chairs. Zero modifications required.

02
Sensors take over

IR and ultrasonic sensors constantly scan the environment — detecting obstacles, drop-offs, and incline angles — feeding real-time data to the onboard controller.

03
You stay in control

Switch between electric and manual modes anytime. Use voice commands, the Bluetooth remote, or the high-contrast UI — while SmartDrive Assist handles safety automatically.

About

Built by students.
Built for impact.

SmartDrive Assist is a student-built assistive tech initiative under TechCare Warriors — applying real hardware engineering to a problem that affects 75 million people worldwide.

The project sits at the intersection of servo robotics, inclusive design, and social impact — currently in active prototype development with interest from disability-tech organisations.
